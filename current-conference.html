About the Conference

The QANZIAM Conference brings together applied mathematicians from across Queensland for a day of research presentations, invited talks, and community building. It's a relaxed, collegial event and a great opportunity for students to present their work in a supportive environment.

Each presenter will be allocated 20 minutes: 15 minutes for the presentation, 3 minutes for questions, and 2 minutes for changeover to the next speaker.

Whether you're an experienced researcher or a student just starting out, we'd love to see you there. Students are especially encouraged to submit an abstract and give a talk. Please feel free to forward this to anyone who may be interested — the conference is open to everyone.

Call for Abstracts

We welcome contributed talks from researchers and students at all career stages. Each talk is allocated 20 minutes (15 min presentation + 3 min questions + 2 min changeover).

To submit an abstract:

  1. Prepare a short abstract (150–300 words)
  2. Email it to d.harman@griffith.edu.au
  3. Include your name, affiliation, and whether you are a student
