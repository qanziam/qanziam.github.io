Conference Presentations: Tips, Tricks, and Common Pitfalls

Patrick Grant

Presenter

Shahak Kuba

Co-author

This seminar covers some practical tips, tricks, and tools to help you improve your conference presentations. We'll cover slide formatting, what to include (and what to leave out), visualisation theory, and strategies for making your slides more accessible. Using real examples of poorly designed slides that I have presented in the past, we'll critique and workshop them to enhance clarity and impact. You'll also gain insights from conference judges on what they look for when evaluating presentations for awards and prizes.
