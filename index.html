What is QANZIAM?

QANZIAM is the Queensland branch of ANZIAM (Australian and New Zealand Industrial and Applied Mathematics), a division of the Australian Mathematical Society.

We have a special focus on supporting HDR students. We bring students together with academics and industry practitioners who share a passion for applying mathematics to real-world problems. From fluid dynamics to data science, mathematical biology to operations research, our members work across the full spectrum of applied mathematics.
